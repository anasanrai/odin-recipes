Burger

Description

A juicy, flavorful burger perfect for cookouts or quick dinners.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Shape and season the beef patties.
  2. Grill or fry the patties until cooked.
  3. Toast buns, assemble with toppings, and serve.
