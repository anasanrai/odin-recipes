Cupcakes

Description

Light and fluffy cupcakes, perfect for birthdays and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Mix all ingredients until smooth
  3. Pour into cupcake liners
  4. Bake 18–20 minutes
  5. Cool and frost
