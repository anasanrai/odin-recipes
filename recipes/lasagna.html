Delicious Lasagna

A classic, cheesy lasagna that’s perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 700 ml tomato sauce
  • 250 g ricotta cheese
  • 200 g shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190 °C (375 °F).
  2. Cook noodles until al dente; drain.
  3. Brown beef in a pan and stir in tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Bake 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let rest 5 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
