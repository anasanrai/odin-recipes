Blueberry Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes bursting with fresh blueberries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all‑purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Whisk together dry ingredients.
  2. Beat in egg and milk until smooth.
  3. Fold in blueberries.
  4. Cook ¼‑cup scoops on hot griddle until golden.
  5. Flip, cook other side, then serve warm.
