Simple Homemade Pizza

Crispy crust topped with melty cheese and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch pizza dough
  • 200 ml tomato sauce
  • 150 g mozzarella
  • 50 g pepperoni slices
  • ½ tsp dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 230 °C (450 °F).
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add pepperoni.
  4. Season with oregano.
  5. Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
