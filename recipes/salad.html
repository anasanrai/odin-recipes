Fresh Salad

A fresh and healthy salad with crisp vegetables and a light vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red onion
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
