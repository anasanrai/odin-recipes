Fruit Smoothie

This smoothie is packed with fruits and is perfect for a refreshing treat.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Yogurt
  • Honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy cold.
