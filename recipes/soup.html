Vegetable Soup

A warm and hearty vegetable soup perfect for cold days.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Heat oil and saute onions and garlic.
  3. Add other vegetables and broth.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes. Serve hot.
