Spaghetti

Classic spaghetti pasta with savory tomato sauce and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or plant-based option
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Saute onions and garlic.
  3. Add ground meat and cook.
  4. Mix with tomato sauce and season.
  5. Combine with pasta and serve.
